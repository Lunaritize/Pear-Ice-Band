The Carnival Culture

Carnival has been a traditional cultural festival in many Caribbean territories and Jamaica is no different. Every year, the Mango Carnival in St. Catherine sees thousands of costumed masqueraders parading through the streets of Linstead, dancing to the pulsating sounds of calypso, soca, and reggae, then having the party at Rosemount Guest House. This year of 2023 is no different!

The Pear-Ice Band

The Pear-Ice Mango Carnival Band is one of the most renowned and exciting bands in the festival. With dazzling costumes, energetic performances, and infectious music, we bring the true spirit of Carnival to life.

Join us as we parade through the streets, showcasing our stunning costumes and dancing to the rhythm of the Caribbean. Whether you're an experienced masquerader or new to Carnival, there's a place for everyone in the Pear-Ice Band.

The Sections

This year, our band offers two (2) sections from which our prospective members can choose:

  • Section 1 - Mystic Fire
  • Section 2 - Enchanted Dreams

The cost of the costumes between these two sections differs. Section 1 costs US$ 880 for the costume, while Section 2 costs US$ 1300.

Payment Options

The cost for these costumes can be paid in two different methods:

  1. Full payment - The member pays the full amount at once, at a 12% Discount!
  2. Installments - The member pays in 3 equal installments, at 15% Interest!

How To Apply

Joining the Pear-Ice Mango Carnival Band is easy. Follow the steps below to apply:

  1. Visit our website and navigate to the "How To Apply" section.
  2. Locate and download the designated application form provided.
  3. Complete the application form by filling in the required information accurately.
  4. Save the filled out application form.
  5. Access one of our social media pages listed in the footer of this webpage.
  6. Initiate a direct message to our social media page, ensuring privacy and confidentiality.
  7. Attach the completed application form to the message and send it to us.
  8. Congratulations! You have successfully submitted your application. Kindly await our response and further instructions.

Please note that spaces are limited, and applications are processed on a first-come, first-served basis. Ensure to submit your application and complete the payment as soon as possible to secure your spot in the Pear-Ice Mango Carnival Band.
